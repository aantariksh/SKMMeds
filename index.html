Who is Dr. Kaul and how is she involved?

Dr. Kaul is the founder of SKM Meds and is personally involved with every student. She provides one-on-one guidance and customises teaching based on individual needs, ensuring quality and accountability.

What makes SKM Meds different from other tutoring services?

SKM Meds offers personalised attention, a 100% success record, and a close-knit team of experienced doctors and dentists passionate about mentoring students. We go beyond academics to build confidence and character.

What services do you provide for UCAT preparation?

We provide one-to-one tutoring, a comprehensive question bank, online courses, regular homework sessions, and interactive live sessions—all focused on maximising UCAT performance.

Do you offer help with interviews?

Yes. We offer MMI and panel interview preparation through weekend courses, regular teaching, and mock circuits. Training includes tailored feedback and techniques to boost confidence and structure responses.

Absolutely. We support students preparing for overseas exams such as ORE, PLAB, and SJT. Our programs are tailored to international requirements and delivered online for easy access.

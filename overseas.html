Overseas

Overseas

A certified Overseas exam is required for all the Medicine/Dentistry/Pharmacy graduates who wish to work in the UK . The purpose of the exam is to make sure that the individual is fit to work in the NHS .The results of the test will determine whether the applicant will be granted a work permit



Overseas Exam Preparation

ORE Overseas Registration Exam for Dentists – Preparation offered online/offline.
PLAB Professional and Linguistic Assessments Board – Preparation offered online/offline.
SJT – EU Students Situational Judgment Test – Preparation offered online/offline.
Pharmacy Preparation offered online/offline.
